SCHOOL ZONE INCIDENT MANAGEMENT

Priority Level: HIGH (always elevated near schools)
Active Hours: 07:30-08:30, 14:30-15:30 (school days)

SPECIAL CONSIDERATIONS:
- Speed limits automatically reduced to 20 mph in school zones
- Enhanced pedestrian protection — do not reduce pedestrian crossing time
- Diversion routes must NEVER pass through active school zones
- Deploy school crossing guards to alternate pedestrian routes if needed

SIGNAL MANAGEMENT NEAR SCHOOLS:
- Maintain minimum pedestrian phase (MUTCD Section 4E.06)
- Leading pedestrian intervals (LPI) must not be shortened
- If school zone is within 600m upstream window, prioritize pedestrian safety over vehicle throughput
- No right-turn-on-red during active school hours

INCIDENT DURING SCHOOL HOURS:
1. Notify school district immediately if incident is within 500m of a school
2. Consider early dismissal coordination if road closure affects school bus routes
3. Activate crossing guard at alternate intersections
4. VMS messaging must include "SCHOOL ZONE — SLOW DOWN"

DIVERSION RESTRICTIONS:
- Do not route diverted traffic through residential streets adjacent to schools
- Maximum acceptable vehicle density on school zone streets: 40 veh/km
- If no school-zone-free diversion exists, reduce diversion volume to 15%
