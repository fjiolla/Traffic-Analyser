VMS AND PUBLIC ALERT GUIDELINES

VARIABLE MESSAGE SIGN STANDARDS:
- Maximum 3 lines per message phase
- Maximum 20 characters per line (including spaces)
- ALL CAPS required
- No abbreviations unless universally understood (AVE, BLVD, ST, PKWY)
- Display time: minimum 3 seconds per phase, maximum 2 phases per cycle
- Refresh cycle: 6-8 seconds total

ACCEPTABLE ABBREVIATIONS:
AVE (Avenue), BLVD (Boulevard), ST (Street), PKWY (Parkway),
RD (Road), HWY (Highway), N/S/E/W (directions), MIN (minutes),
ALT (Alternate), RT (Route), CLR (Clear), TEMP (Temporary)

RADIO BROADCAST STANDARDS:
- Duration: 15 seconds (approximately 35-40 words)
- Opening: "Brooklyn traffic advisory" or "[Borough] traffic alert"
- Include: Location, impact, recommended action
- Tone: Professional, calm, urgent but not alarming
- Repeat key information: Location mentioned twice
- End with: "Seek alternate routes" or specific diversion

SOCIAL MEDIA STANDARDS:
- Character limit: 280 (Twitter/X compatibility)
- Required hashtag: #BrooklynTraffic
- Include: Severity emoji (⚠️ or 🚨), location, impact, advice
- No speculation about cause or injuries
- Link to 511NY for real-time updates
- Posting schedule: Initial post + update every 15 minutes until resolved
- Tone: Factual, professional, empathetic

TIMING PRIORITY:
1. VMS signs: Activate within 60 seconds of alert decision
2. Social media: Post within 90 seconds
3. Radio: Broadcast within 3 minutes (requires studio upload)
