HAZARDOUS MATERIAL SPILL PROTOCOL

Priority Level: CRITICAL
Coordinate with: FDNY HazMat, NYPD ESU, DEP

DETECTION:
- Sudden speed drop to near-zero in localized area
- May or may not involve vehicle collision
- Confirm via camera feed or on-scene report

IMMEDIATE ACTIONS:
1. Establish 500m exclusion zone (wider than standard collision)
2. Close ALL road segments within exclusion zone
3. Diversion radius: minimum 1.5km from spill location
4. Alert FDNY HazMat team — estimated response time 8-12 minutes

SIGNAL MANAGEMENT:
- All intersections within 500m: switch to flash mode (red)
- Intersections at 500m-1km: extend green AWAY from incident
- Create one-way outbound flow on all radial streets from incident
- Do NOT create green waves toward the incident (prevents rubbernecking)

DIVERSION:
- Use only routes confirmed to be UPWIND from spill
- Coordinate with FDNY for wind direction updates every 15 minutes
- Consider complete sector closure if material is airborne
- No pedestrian routing through affected area

PUBLIC ALERTS:
- VMS: "ROAD CLOSED - HAZMAT INCIDENT - SEEK ALT ROUTE"
- Radio: Include shelter-in-place advisory if airborne contaminant
- Social media: Link to NYC OEM updates, include wind direction
- Duration: Minimum 2-hour expected closure (adjust based on HazMat assessment)

DOCUMENTATION:
- Record exact spill location, estimated quantity, material type
- Track wind direction and changes
- Log exclusion zone boundaries and modifications
- Coordinate with DEP for environmental cleanup timeline
